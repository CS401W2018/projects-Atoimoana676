Malakai Fekitoa

Malakai Fekitoa was born and raised in Tonga, where he attended Liahona High School and began his journey in rugby. With hard work and dedication, he rose to the top, playing for some of the best teams in the world and proudly representing Tonga on the global stage. Despite his success, Malakai remains humble and deeply connected to his roots. He is a shining example to young people in the islands, proving that with determination, they can achieve anything. Respected for his kindness and strong cultural values, he continues to honor his heritage and family, staying true to who he is no matter where life takes him.

Teams played for icludes:
Malakai played for many of the worlds top teams including All Blacks, Tonga, France, Highlanders.
Accomplishments:
He was able to make it to the worlds #1 team which is the Allblacks and being a key player for them as well as the highlanders in winning the championships for these teams.
Places lived:
He lived while playing rugby in many places around the world including France, Tonga, New Zealand, etc
